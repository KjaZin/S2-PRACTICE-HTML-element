Cambodia(Khmer), officially the Kingdom of Cambodia, is a country in Mainland Southeast Asia. It borders Thailand to the northwest, Laos to the north, Vietnam to the east, and has a coastline along the Gulf of Thailand.
Cambodia spans an area of 181,035 square kilometres, and has a population of about 17 milions. Its capital and the most populous city is Phnom Penh, have population more than 2 million people, approximately 14% of the Cambodia population with 67,846 square kilimetres.
